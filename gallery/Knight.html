Knight (2016)

This was a fun project to make some art-work for my sons' bedroom. We put up seveal of wall decals from e-glue.fr in the "Knights vs. Dragons" theme and wanted a bed-spread to go with them. This knight was drawn in a similar style. This image was created in Inkscape as an SVG, so that it could be exported for an image that was 4.5 feet tall.
